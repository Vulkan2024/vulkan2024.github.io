Who are we?

Team Vulkan F1 is a team from Guanacaste Costa Rica full of talented young people eager to learn and participate in steam racing.

To promote inclusion, we have a diverse and varied team, where we all live together in a respectful and equal environment. In addition, including our representative volcano with its strength and grandeur, it represents the Formula 1 that we are so passionate and interested in.
