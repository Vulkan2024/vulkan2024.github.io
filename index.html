F1 in Schools

F1 in Schools is a global STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics) focused event aimed at middle and high school students. In this exciting competition, teams of 3 to 6 students embark on the challenge of creating a miniature car using the official F1 model block, and applying Computer Aided Design (CAD) and Computer Aided Manufacturing (CAM) tools.

Mission

Learning and fostering STEAM science and passion for motorsports...
